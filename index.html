Welcome to the Bridge

The Bridge Lutheran Church is an LCMS Lutheran Church in Washington, Missouri. It is the daughter church of New Beginnings Lutheran Church in Pacific, Missouri and our first services were held in Washington on April 25, 2010. Leading the church is Pastor Andy Yount.

Worship is held each Sunday at 9:30 am at 2226 Highway A in Washington across from Miller's Grill.

  • What can I expect when I come?

    • Each week, you'll join 75 to 150 others in a vibrant worship service that will last between 60 and 90 minutes. You'll hear a law & gospel message utilizing a multimedia presentation, with worship consistiing of current contemporary Christian praise music led by our praise team.

  • What should I wear?

    • Most people who worship with us wear casual clothing. You should feel free to wear what is comfortable for you.

  • Where are you located?

    • We are just south of Washington on Highway A. Take Highway 100 to Highway A and turn south. Go 7/10s of a mile to 2226 which is on the left, just past the A-way storage units, directly across the street from Miller's Grill. For more precise, check out the map below!

  • What about my kids?

    • We have an age appropriate class for children who are four years old through second grade, that is held during the sermon. They will be led through various activities, and learn stories from the Bible. For children ages three and under, we have a 'wiggle room' available where volunteer members are available during the service.

  • First time visiting?

    • We ask you to take a quick survey after your first, and second worship with us. You can find them here for the first, and here for the second.
